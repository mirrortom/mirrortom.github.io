比较简单的图标,外侧圆圈就是i标记,边框变成圆角.里面的斜杠用:before,旋转45度得到.容器使用inline-flex,项目居中对齐.

完整样式


  .ico-disabled
      display:inline-flex
      justify-content:center
      align-items:center
      width: 1em
      height: 1em
      border: .08em solid currentColor
      border-radius: 50%
    
      &:before
          content: ''
          display: inline-block
          width: 1em
          height: .08em
          background-color:currentColor
          transform: rotate(45deg)
